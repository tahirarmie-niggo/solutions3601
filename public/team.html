Working with us

How your file is handled

Will I deal with the same person throughout?

Yes. One case officer owns your file from the first session to departure, and you get their direct number. Specialists step in for their part — attestation, financial documents — but you are not passed between desks or asked to explain your situation again.

Who do I contact if my counsellor is unavailable?

Every file is visible to the whole office, so anyone can pick it up and see exactly where it stands. During office hours, WhatsApp usually gets the fastest answer regardless of who is in.

Can I ask for a specific counsellor?

You can, and we will accommodate it where the workload allows. That said, files are normally assigned by specialism — medical applications go to the Europe desk, Canadian visas to the visa desk — because that usually serves you better than a name.

Do you charge for the first meeting?

No. The first consultation is free whether you proceed with us or not, and we will tell you honestly if we do not think we can help.

Can my parents sit in on the session?

Please bring them. Most of the questions that matter — funding, timelines, what happens if a visa is refused — are the ones parents ask, and it saves everyone a second conversation later.
