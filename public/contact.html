FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the initial counselling really free? +

Yes, 100% free. Our initial consultation has no fees or obligations. We believe every student deserves honest advice before making a decision.

How long does the process take from consultation to visa? +

It varies by destination and intake. Typically 3–6 months from consultation to visa approval. We always plan well ahead of deadlines to avoid any rush.

What if my visa gets rejected? +

We have a 98% visa success rate but in rare cases of rejection, we analyze the reason and help you reapply with a stronger application at no extra charge.

Do you help with accommodation and post-arrival support? +

Yes! We assist with finding safe and affordable accommodation near your university. We also provide a pre-departure briefing and remain available after your arrival.

Can you help with scholarship applications? +

Absolutely. We actively look for scholarship opportunities for every student and help you craft strong personal statements and scholarship applications to maximize your chances.
