We do not win every case

Our approval rate is 98% on files we agree to submit — which is not the same as 98% of everyone who walks in. We turn cases away when the profile or the funds are not there yet, and we tell those students what would need to change.

If a consultancy tells you every application succeeds, ask what happens to the ones that do not.
