Which level applies to you

Undergraduate

After FSc, A-Levels or an equivalent. Most countries need IBCC equivalence before an application can even be assessed, so start that early.

Typical: 3–4 years · IELTS 6.0

Postgraduate

After a Pakistani bachelor degree. A 16-year education requirement applies in several countries, which a three-year BA or BSc may not meet on its own.

Typical: 1–2 years · IELTS 6.5

Scholarships & funding

Merit awards, university fee waivers and country schemes such as DAAD or regional Italian grants. We will tell you honestly whether your marks are competitive.

Before you apply

Four things that decide your shortlist

IBCC equivalence

Your FSc or A-Level results converted into the receiving country's grading system. Universities cannot assess you without it.

Start 3 months early
English test

IELTS is accepted almost everywhere. Some universities waive it if your prior education was English-medium, but do not assume.

Valid 2 years
16 years of education

Several countries require this for masters entry. A three-year bachelor may need a bridging year or additional qualification.

Check per country
Proof of funds

The figure differs by country and must usually be held for a set period before you file. This often decides the destination.

28 days minimum, UK
