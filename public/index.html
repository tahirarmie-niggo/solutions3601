The process

Four stages, in this order

01

Free consultation

You tell us your marks, budget and timeline. We tell you honestly which countries are realistic and which aren't.

02

Profile assessment & shortlist

We review transcripts, IELTS scores and gaps in your academic record, then build a shortlist of universities you'll get into.

03

Applications & attestation

Documents go for HEC and IBCC attestation while applications are submitted. Both run in parallel so you don't lose an intake.

04

Visa filing & departure

Financial documents prepared, interview practised, visa filed. Then a pre-departure briefing before you fly.
